# Product contract

## The job

Help someone understand pitch language without losing their place in the work.

## Inputs

A query, category filter, selected term, or related-term navigation choice.

## Outputs

- fuzzy-ranked matching terms;
- a plain definition;
- why the term matters and what it changes;
- common confusions and related concepts;
- a visual and textual constellation of nearby terms;
- an optional deterministic surprise route into a valid definition.

## Editorial status

Definitions are practical editorial guidance, not universal laws. Meanings can vary by market, route, company, country, discipline, and stage. Current written requirements and qualified specialist advice outrank a general dictionary.

## Constellation status

The constellation is a deterministic navigation aid. Curated relationships and transparent similarity rules place terms near one another; it does not claim an objective ontology or use hidden inference.

The physics opener is optional navigation, not a gate. Touch, pointer, keyboard, and reduced-motion routes expose the same definitions.

## Privacy

Queries and navigation remain in the browser. The production tool has no account, database, analytics, upload, or runtime AI call.
