# Product contract

## The job

Help someone understand pitch language without losing their place in the work.

## Inputs

A query, category filter, selected term, or related-term navigation choice.

## Outputs

- fuzzy-ranked matching terms;
- a plain definition;
- why the term matters and what it changes;
- common confusions and related concepts;
- a visual and textual constellation of nearby terms;
- an optional deterministic surprise route into a valid definition.

## Editorial status

Definitions are practical editorial guidance, not universal laws. Meanings can vary by market, route, company, country, discipline, and stage. Current written requirements and qualified specialist advice outrank a general dictionary.

## Galaxy status

The galaxy is a deterministic navigation aid. Explicit related terms, common confusions, reciprocal links, curated working groups, shared language, and category proximity determine its routes. It does not claim an objective ontology or use hidden inference.

Star size represents recorded connection degree. Brightness represents closeness to the current term. Neither represents truth, quality, popularity, or universal importance.

The galaxy is optional navigation, not a gate. Search remains the direct route. Touch, pointer, keyboard, and reduced-motion journeys expose the same definitions and textual related-term list.

Physics may change position and velocity; it may not change editorial evidence, relationship rank, definitions, search results, or the current term without a deliberate user choice. Reduced motion removes momentum, twinkling, shooting stars, and animated travel.

## Privacy

Queries and navigation remain in the browser. The production tool has no account, database, analytics, upload, or runtime AI call.
